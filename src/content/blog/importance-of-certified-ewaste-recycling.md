Electronic waste is growing rapidly as technology advances and devices become obsolete faster. Managing this waste responsibly is crucial for protecting the environment and public health. Certified e-waste recycling offers a reliable way to handle electronic waste safely and efficiently.

## Why Certified E-Waste Recycling Matters

Many electronics contain hazardous materials — lead, mercury, cadmium, and flame retardants — that can leach into soil and groundwater when thrown in a landfill. Certified recyclers follow strict standards to ensure these materials are handled correctly, keeping them out of the environment and out of your community.

## What Happens to Your Electronics

When you drop off electronics at a certified facility, they go through a careful sorting process. Items that still work get refurbished or resold. Parts that can be salvaged are recovered. What's left goes to a certified end-of-life processor for safe material recovery.

No shortcuts. No dumping. Just responsible handling from start to finish.
